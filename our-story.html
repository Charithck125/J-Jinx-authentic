Our Story

J&Jinx Authentic Virgin Oil began with a simple idea — to share the natural purity of Sri Lanka’s coconuts with the world, starting with our second home: Sweden. Founded by two lifelong friends, Charith Katunayakage and Tharindu Balasooriya, our journey is rooted in authenticity, island heritage, and a deep love for organic living.

As Sri Lankans living in Sweden, we saw how rare it was to find coconut oil that was not just labeled “organic,” but actually felt close to the source — pure, clean, and deeply cared for. So we partnered with local farmers back home who harvest coconuts the traditional way and cold-press the oil without chemicals, heat, or shortcuts.

Each jar of J&Jinx Virgin Coconut Oil is produced in small batches to preserve its natural properties. We pack it in 100 ml eco-glass jars — perfect for skincare, hair nourishment, baby care, or daily wellness rituals. From sourcing to shipping, we handle each step with personal attention, because your trust means everything to us.

Whether you’re a skincare enthusiast, a soap maker, or simply someone who values clean ingredients, we created this product for you. This isn’t just oil — it’s a story, a connection, and a promise of purity.
